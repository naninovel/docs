Register Naninovel

Thank you for purchasing Naninovel! Registering will allow you to access developer support on the Naninovel discord server.

Fill in the invoice and discord ID fields in the form at the bottom of the page and press 'Register' button to perform the registration.

You can find your invoice (order number) in the purchase confirmation email sent by Unity. If you didn't receive the invoice or can't find it, contact Unity support.

To find your discord ID enable developer mode in the Discord client (user settings -> appearance -> developer mode), then right-click your portrait in the server's member list and choose 'Copy ID'. Check out this detailed guide or video if you have troubles finding the ID. Don't forget to join Naninovel discord server before the registration!

When the registration completes you should automatically get a 'Verified User' role on the server and access to the #support channel. If you'll have any issues during the registration feel free to contact the support.
